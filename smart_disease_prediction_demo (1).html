    System architecture
    1
    Logistic Regression (sklearn)
    Trained on 303 heart disease records. Predicts binary outcome (disease / no disease) with probability score after StandardScaler normalization.
    2
    Apriori Algorithm (custom built-in)
    Mines frequent symptom co-occurrence patterns from binarized features (age >55, high cholesterol, etc.). Generates association rules with support, confidence, and lift metrics.
    3
    Expert System (rule-based)
    Converts ML prediction + raw vitals into actionable medical recommendations. Calculates risk score and outputs LOW / MODERATE / HIGH risk with tailored advice.
    Dataset
    303 simulated patient records · 13 features · Binary target (disease/no disease) · 80/20 train-test split · Features: age, sex, chest pain type, resting BP, cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG, max heart rate, exercise-induced angina, ST depression, slope, CA vessels, thalassemia type.
